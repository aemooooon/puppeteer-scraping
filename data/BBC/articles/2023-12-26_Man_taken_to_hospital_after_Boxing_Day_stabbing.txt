A man has been taken to hospital after he was stabbed in the early hours of Boxing Day, police said.
Bedfordshire Police dispatched officers to Woodcock Road, Luton, at about 03:10 GMT on Tuesday.
They remained at the scene throughout the morning and into the afternoon as they investigated the reported stabbing.
"The victim has been taken to hospital to be treated for his injuries," a police spokesperson said.
East of England Ambulance Service was contacted for comment.
